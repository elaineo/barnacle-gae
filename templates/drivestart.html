What Happens During a Delivery?

 

After you have confirmed a request, you will pick up your delivery item either from the sender's location or at Barnacle's warehouse. The location will be agreed upon before confirmation.

 

Make sure you have the receiver's contact information or dropoff instructions before embarking on your delivery!

 


 
Confirm Delivery

If your final destination is a Barnacle office, we will verify and confirm your delivery immediately.

Otherwise, a "Confirm Delivery" link will appear at the top of your Account page. After releasing your delivery to the recipient, use this button to notify us and we will verify the delivery with the sender. Once delivery has been confirmed by both parties, your payment will be credited to your account.

 

For your own security, we encourage you to obtain a photo of one of the following:

  • A street view of the delivery item at its final destination
  • The recipient with the delivery

 

Coming Soon

In the next few weeks, Barnacle will be available for Android and iOS for real-time tracking and confirmation.
The mobile app will also allow you to obtain a signature confirmation from the recipient.
