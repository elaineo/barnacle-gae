Why do I need to connect with Facebook?

Barnacle is a community. Having a Facebook account establishes an identity for our users and shows others that you are a real person. However, we do understand that some of our users really don't like connecting with their Facebook accounts. If that is you, sign up here and we will verify your identity to set you up with a username and password.
