Barnacle ♥ Heartland
January 8, 2014
Posted by: Gerrit

Barnacle currently operates best along I-5, servicing the cities of Seattle, Portland, Sacramento/SF Bay Area, LA/San Diego, and Las Vegas. This hasn't slowed our eager Barnacle driver community, who have posted routes across the country.

In December 2013, I had the pleasure of taking a cross-country Barnacle delivery. Visiting family across the country, it's not really a surprise our marketplace is thriving in the heartland. The Hall family proved to be great early Barnacle users.
