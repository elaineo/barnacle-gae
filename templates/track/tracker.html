Barnacle makes it easy for drivers to provide real-time tracking information en route with the Barnacle Driver Tracker.

Here's how it works:

Sync your routes with the server. Manage your routes by selecting which ones you are actively driving at any time. The other routes remain inactive.


Or, create a new route.



Barnacle will generate a page that you can share with your customers and friends so that they can see your updates.



Set your route to give updates at regular intervals.


When you arrive at your destination, the recipient will give you a confirmation code. Confirm your delivery and get paid instantly!


The Barnacle Driver Tracker is available for iOS in the App Store and for Android in the Google Play Store.
