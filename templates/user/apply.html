How it Works

Barnacle is a community delivery network that matches drivers with people who need to send parcels or receive shipments. Bulky or fragile items may be uneconomical to ship. Flying with luggage is a hassle. We are here to help!

Barnacle drivers may be regular commuters or individuals going on a road trip. Users are linked by their Facebook accounts to ensure that you are working with people you trust. For added security, we offer an option for insurance coverage on each delivery.
