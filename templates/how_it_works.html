Why should I use Barnacle instead of USPS?
Barnacle is a community. The driver will work with you to arrange a delivery schedule so you don't have to worry about missed deliveries or infonotices. We provide real-time tracking and look out for our drivers to help them provide you with the best possible service.

As an added bonus, your items get their own private seats or trunk space. This means you don't have to worry about carefully boxing or packaging your shipments.
What can I send using Barnacle?
Anything, as long as the driver approves your request.
I don't have any packages to send. Can I use Barnacle to hitch a ride?
You may arrange a ride with our drivers by searching for a driver or submitting a request. Please note that Barnacle does not provide insurance for human cargo.
How do I know my stuff is in safe hands?
Users are required to link their Facebook accounts to establish a community of trust. You are encouraged to inspect driver profiles for shared social networks.
For additional peace of mind, drivers who have been verified by the Barnacle team can provide insurance on your delivery for up to $500. These drivers are identified with an icon on their profile:
Higher coverage is available by arrangement.
I can't find any drivers traveling to my destination. What should I do?
Please submit a delivery request. We will notify you if we find an available driver. When we find a match, we will send you a link to confirm your reservation.

How are payments handled?
Payments are made at the time of reservation and held in escrow. Barnacle will disburse payment to the driver's account upon delivery confirmation.
Am I required to use Barnacle's payment system?
Nope. Drivers and senders are welcome to arrange their own payment methods. However, using our escrow service gives added security for the sender because drivers are required to provide their bank account information in order to receive payment.

Also, Barnacle's escrow service is required for insured deliveries.
How does insurance work?
If you are sending an insured delivery, we will document your shipment at the time of pickup, or dropoff if you are bringing your item to Barnacle. Insurance obligations are terminated when the recipient accepts the delivery at the destination.
Is there a fee for dropping off or picking up deliveries at the Barnacle office?
No. In fact, we encourage this for the security of our users. For more information about our locations, click here.
